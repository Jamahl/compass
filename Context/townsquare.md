# TownSquare

## Overview
TownSquare is a civic-engagement platform that reconnects local government with the residents it serves. City halls publish agendas, permit notices, and budget line items; residents subscribe by street, topic, or issue and respond through structured feedback channels that feed directly into council workflows. Think "Nextdoor, but with your actual city clerk on the other end."

## Thesis
Municipal communication is broken in both directions. Cities broadcast via PDFs buried on `.gov` sites no one reads; residents vent on Facebook groups that elected officials do not monitor. The result is low trust, low turnout, and reactive policymaking. TownSquare becomes the default civic layer by earning trust on both sides — credible government content on one end, structured resident voice on the other.
