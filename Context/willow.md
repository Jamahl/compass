# Willow

## Overview
Willow is an AI note-taking assistant purpose-built for licensed therapists and mental-health clinicians. It listens (with consent) to 1:1 therapy sessions, produces SOAP-format progress notes within minutes, and drafts insurance-ready billing summaries. Clinicians review and edit before anything leaves the device.

## Thesis
Therapists spend 6–10 unbilled hours per week on notes. Generic ambient scribes (Abridge, Nuance DAX) are tuned for physical medicine and miss the clinical nuance of talk therapy — risk language, therapeutic alliance cues, treatment-plan alignment. A vertical product that speaks the language of CBT, DBT, and EMDR wins this segment.
